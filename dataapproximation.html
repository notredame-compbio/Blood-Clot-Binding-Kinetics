% k02  unloading breaken rate of long-duration bond LR_2
% f1   KbT/f1 is The  minimal adhesion bond length of short-duration bond LR_1
% f2   KbT/f2 is The  minimal adhesion bond length of long-duration bond LR_2
% f12  kBT/F_12 is the distance between the two  minima LR_1 and  LR_2
